Preparing for an interview for a **Python Automation & AI Solutions Specialist** role requires a blend of technical depth, architectural reasoning, and a clear presentation of your "Triple-Threat" career strategy. With only one week, your preparation must be surgical, focusing on your flagship projects and the "Enterprise-Grade" decisions you've already codified.

### **The 7-Day Interview Intensive Plan**

**Day 1: The Strategic Narrative & Flagship Project**
*   **Focus:** Master the "Why" behind your **Enterprise E-Commerce Platform**.
*   **Tasks:** Review your **Architecture Decision Records (ADRs)**. Practice explaining why you chose a **Modular Monolith** over microservices for the MVP to balance speed and maintainability.
*   **Outcome:** Be able to articulate your "Triple-Threat" goal: Remote Job + Freelancing + Solution Architect path.

**Day 2: Backend & API Mastery (FastAPI)**
*   **Focus:** Deep dive into the **FastAPI** layer.
*   **Tasks:** Study the implementation of **JWT Authentication**, **RBAC (Role-Based Access Control)**, and **Stateless APIs**.
*   **Outcome:** Explain how you use **Swagger/OpenAPI** for real-time documentation and the **Service Layer Pattern** to keep code decoupled.

**Day 3: Data Layer & Caching (PostgreSQL & Redis)**
*   **Focus:** Database schema design and performance.
*   **Tasks:** Review your **ER Diagrams** for the E-Commerce platform (Users, Products, Orders, Inventory). Explain the significance of using **UUIDs** and **Timestamps**.
*   **Outcome:** Defend your use of **Redis** for high-performance product caching to achieve <1s response times.

**Day 4: AI Integration & Specialist Modules**
*   **Focus:** How you integrate AI into business workflows.
*   **Tasks:** Review the **Document AI** (OCR + LLM) and **AI Reporting** (Pandas + Plotly) project specs.
*   **Outcome:** Explain the "AI Roadmap"—how you design for future AI features (like recommendation engines) even during initial development.

**Day 5: Infrastructure & DevOps (Docker & AWS)**
*   **Focus:** Environment parity and deployment.
*   **Tasks:** Review your **Docker Compose** setup and the plan for **AWS deployment** (EC2, RDS, S3).
*   **Outcome:** Discuss your **"Definition of Done"**: Every module must be **dockerized, documented, and deployed**.

**Day 6: Behavioral & Portfolio Demo Scripts**
*   **Focus:** Soft skills and demonstration.
*   **Tasks:** Walk through your **Demo Scripts** (e.g., Login → Browse → Checkout). Prepare stories for "Human Responsibilities" like decision-making and accountability.
*   **Outcome:** Convert your **Resume Bullet Points** and **LinkedIn Build Updates** into verbal "Success Stories".

**Day 7: Mock Interviews & Final Polish**
*   **Focus:** Confidence and "Solution Architect" presence.
*   **Tasks:** Use the AI prompt below to simulate technical challenges. Review your **Skills Matrix** to identify and pivot away from any remaining gaps.
